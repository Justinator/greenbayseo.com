    body: >-
      For 11 years, Kurt has overseen purchasing with a commitment to sourcing materials that meet Wisconsin Homes’ strict standards. He is known for his deep product knowledge and calm problem-solving, especially when decisions need to be made quickly and accurately. 

      
      Kurt believes listening is the foundation of good service—understanding what the team needs, what the builder requires and what the homeowner expects. He selects materials not on price alone, but on longevity and supplier reputation. Knowing the quality of the homes Wisconsin Homes produces motivates him every day, along with the opportunity to help build a strong team dedicated to that standard. 


      **Outside the Office:** Kurt spends much of his free time hunting and fishing, tackling remodeling projects like bathrooms and cabins and grilling just about anything—from steaks and ribs to venison loins.
