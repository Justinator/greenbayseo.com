      As President, Paul is deeply involved in steering both the sales and manufacturing sides of Wisconsin Homes with steady intention. He is proud of how the company has stayed grounded in its founding values while continuing to refine how homes are designed, built and delivered. Rather than chasing constant change, Paul focuses on thoughtful improvements that make the process easier for today’s busy homeowners. 


      Paul believes customer satisfaction comes from combining local support during design and pricing with a proven building process. By continually asking how Wisconsin Homes can offer greater quality and convenience, he helps ensure every homeowner receives a home built to last. His motivation comes from knowing that trust is earned over time, and every home must reflect that responsibility.
