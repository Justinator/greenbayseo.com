    body: >-
      Brian brings decades of housing industry experience to Wisconsin Homes, having worked in the field since 1988 and joining the team in 2016 after serving in the U.S. Army. He is proud to help families build well-crafted, affordable custom homes where they can grow and create lasting memories. He also plays a key role in developing and maintaining the systems that allow builders and customers to clearly design features, select finishes and ensure those decisions are accurately communicated to production. 


      Brian approaches every project by viewing the process through the customer’s eyes, knowing that building a home can feel overwhelming. He helps simplify decisions by offering a well-curated selection of options that reflect current trends without being overly complex. Brian wants homeowners to know that Wisconsin Homes builds to the same standards as site-built homes—often higher—while engineering each home to withstand transportation and placement. His commitment to quality and attention to detail drives his work from initial planning through final completion. 


      **Outside the Office:** Brian spends as much time outdoors as possible, and life with four dogs at home keeps him plenty busy.
