    body: >-
      With more than two decades of engineering experience and a key role in sales and design, Caelyn brings both technical expertise and a strong understanding of how homeowners want their spaces to feel. She collaborates with builders and customers on layouts, cabinetry and design selections, helping turn ideas into homes that truly reflect how people live. 
      

      She created and maintains the Homeowners Selection Guide, which simplifies the customization process. Caelyn has also shaped the look and feel of Wisconsin Homes’ displays since 2011. She highlights fresh design trends and the craftsmanship that goes into each model. As a Wisconsin Homes homeowner herself, she takes pride in the strength and comfort these homes offer. 


      **Outside the Office:** Caelyn enjoys hiking with her husband and dog, watching movies, vacationing in the mountains with family and never saying no to great pizza.
