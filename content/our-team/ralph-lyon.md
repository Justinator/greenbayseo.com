    body: >-
      With 29 years on the sales team, Ralph brings a deep understanding of both home design and the Wisconsin Homes product. Early in his career, he worked closely with builders to create custom floor plans that transformed ideas into functional, livable layouts. Seeing those homes come to life, and the excitement it created for customers, remains one of the most rewarding parts of his work. 
      

      Today, Ralph serves as a trusted resource across the company, drawing on decades of product knowledge to support builders and fellow team members. He helps streamline the homebuilding process by offering competitively priced, high-quality options that reflect current trends. Ralph is motivated by being part of a third-generation, family-run company that values its people, stands behind its homes and treats both customers and employees like family. 


      **Outside the Office:** Ralph enjoys spending time with his grandchildren, strengthening his faith walk, and working in his orchard and vegetable gardens.
