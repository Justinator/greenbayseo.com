      As Chairman of the Board, Mark helps guide Wisconsin Homes by staying true to the principles the company was founded on in 1965: God, Family and Business. Those values continue to shape how decisions are made today, keeping the focus on doing what’s right for both customers and employees.


      Mark believes strongly in the power of building homes in a controlled environment using proven, repeatable processes. That belief has driven Wisconsin Homes to consistently deliver homes known for strength and lasting value. What motivates him is a simple but powerful standard: every home built must be one the company is proud to put its name on—no compromises, ever.
