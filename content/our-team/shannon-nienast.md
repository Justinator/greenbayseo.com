      As a third-generation leader, Shannon is committed to strengthening the legacy Wisconsin Homes is built on—quality craftsmanship, structural integrity and an intelligent building process. He is focused on modernizing the experience for today’s homeowners by giving them tools that alleviate the stress often associated with building a new home. 
      

      Shannon believes that the best way to serve customers is to blend innovation with the core principles that have guided Wisconsin Homes for decades. That means standing behind guaranteed pricing, ensuring customers know exactly what they’re getting and leading a team dedicated to delivering results. Every home reflects the standard Wisconsin Homes is known for. 


      **Outside the Office:** Shannon enjoys golfing without keeping score, traveling anywhere there’s a beach, and sitting down to a good homemade lasagna—though mountain destinations are a close second.
